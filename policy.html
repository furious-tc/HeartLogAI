# Privacy Policy HeartLog AI **Last Updated: September 20, 2025** ## 1. Introduction HeartLog AI ("we", "us", "our") takes the protection of your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information about you when using our HeartLog AI mobile application. ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Information You Provide - **Profile Information:** age, height, weight - **Medical Data:** blood pressure readings, heart rate measurements - **Notes:** text notes accompanying measurements - **Settings:** measurement unit preferences, notification settings ### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information - **Usage Data:** how you use the application - **Technical Information:** iOS version, device model - **Logs:** error information and performance data ### 2.3 HealthKit Data - **Reading:** blood pressure and heart rate readings - **Writing:** new measurements to the Health app - **Synchronization:** data between the application and HealthKit ## 3. How We Use Information We use collected information for: ### 3.1 Core Functions - **Health Tracking:** storing and analyzing your medical indicators - **AI Analysis:** providing personalized recommendations - **Charts and Trends:** visualizing your data - **Notifications:** measurement reminders ### 3.2 Service Improvement - **Feature Development:** creating new capabilities - **Optimization:** improving application performance - **Support:** helping resolve issues ### 3.3 AI Cardiologist - **Query Processing:** analyzing your health-related questions - **Personalization:** considering your parameters (age, height, weight) - **Recommendations:** providing advice based on data ## 4. Information Sharing with Third Parties ### 4.1 OpenAI API - **Purpose:** processing AI Cardiologist queries and generating insights - **Data:** your questions, medical indicators, profile information - **Protection:** data transmitted over secure connections ### 4.2 HealthKit - **Purpose:** synchronization with the Health app - **Data:** blood pressure and heart rate readings - **Control:** you can disable synchronization in settings ### 4.3 We DO NOT share data with: - Advertising networks - Marketing agencies - Other applications without your consent ## 5. Data Storage and Security ### 5.1 Local Storage - **CoreData:** all data stored locally on your device - **Encryption:** data protected by built-in iOS mechanisms - **Backup:** data included in iCloud backups (if enabled) ### 5.2 Data Protection - **Device Security:** use Touch ID/Face ID for protection - **Regular Updates:** we regularly update the application - **Limited Access:** only you have access to your data ## 6. Your Rights ### 6.1 Data Access - **Viewing:** you can view all your data in the application - **Export:** ability to export data (Premium feature) - **Deletion:** you can delete all data through settings ### 6.2 Permission Management - **HealthKit:** you can disable access in iOS settings - **Notifications:** you can disable in application settings - **Camera:** you can disable for OCR and heart rate measurement functions ### 6.3 Consent Withdrawal - **At any time:** you can withdraw consent for data processing - **Account Deletion:** deletion of all application data - **Stop Using:** simply delete the application ## 7. Children's Data HeartLog AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ## 8. International Transfers Your data may be transferred and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States, where OpenAI servers are located. ## 9.
